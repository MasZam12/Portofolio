About Me

Hi! My name is Ilham Zam Adi Wiranata, and I’m someone who loves combining creativity, logic, and technology to bring ideas to life. With a degree in Mechatronics and Artificial Intelligence, I’ve had the privilege of working on various projects that challenge me to think outside the box and develop innovative solutions. I’m particularly fascinated by how machines can be programmed to replicate human intelligence and how automation can simplify complex tasks. My work involves blending mechanical systems with electronic components and intelligent software to create systems that are efficient, reliable, and adaptable. What drives me is the opportunity to solve problems—whether it’s creating a robot that performs precise tasks, developing a monitoring system for environmental conditions, or designing an AI model that predicts outcomes. Every project is a chance to learn, grow, and contribute something meaningful. But my passion doesn’t stop at technology. I’m also interested in how these advancements affect people and communities. I believe technology should be inclusive, accessible, and used responsibly to create a positive impact. In my free time, I enjoy experimenting with new tools and techniques, diving into books about emerging technologies, and brainstorming ideas for future innovations. Life, for me, is a continuous journey of discovery, and I’m excited to see where my curiosity and determination will lead.
